Who should take this course?

People who :

  • Have a high school diploma or university degree who wish to learn to formulate their own cosmetic products
  • are just starting to make their own cosmetic products
  • wanting to improve basic cosmetic science skills
  • looking to make natural skincare formulas and natural haircare formulas using easy-to-source cosmetic ingredients
  • wanting to learn basic cosmetic science before committing to a more professional study program
