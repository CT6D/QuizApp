How it all started...

It all started when the founder created an Instagram account to share her passion for cosmetic science, and creative storytelling in the hope to spread awareness about safe cosmetic products' use in the midst of all influencer-fed myths, misinformation, baseless fear-mongering, and empty market claims.

Problems

However, when followers started asking for product recommendations, she soon noticed how the market was saturated with either:

  • 1

    No science-based, creative formulations but rather repetitive, dull, and boring products with no problem-solving mindset.

  • 2

    Absence of expert opinions, transparency, or a trustworthy source of information in the midst of misinformation,
    false marketing claims, and fear-mongering in the absence of clear regulation.

  • 3

    Lack of a unique brand experience, sense of community, inclusivity, or involvement.

  • 4

    Fast consumption-driven industry and no ecological responsibility.

Our mission

Clementine is a new innovative cosmetics brand that is set out to finally change the cosmetic industry in Algeria by creating new well researched, science backed and creative formulas.
In addition to special focus on providing a credible, trustworthy source of guidance, advice and information to the public by experts in the field through a science communication platform in our effort to give the cosmetic industry that is reined by myths, empty marketing claims, and mediocracy more transparency, accessibility and integrity.

1

Research and critical thinking: Clementine wants to encourage our community to think for themselves, sieve through the myths, question things and get to the truth. This way, we ensure to only use ingredients and formulas that are safe and efficacious and don't feed into fear-mongering fallacies.

2

Community: Clementine desires to create space for people who speak the language of personal care, science, fun and express their zest for life through unique experiences. We want to emphasize this sense of community and promote higher standards of living.

3

Sustainability: In our process to create our own space we want to do our best to respect the space of other creatures in the planet. We at clementine will accompany our customers in their sustainability journey and try to offer the best alternatives using recyclable packaging, encouraging good recycling habits and by sourcing our ingredients as locally as possible to reduce the carbon footprint it takes to ship ingredients worldwide. Some essential materials are not currently produced in our country, but by existing, we're demanding it, it's going to be a long journey but we're committed.
