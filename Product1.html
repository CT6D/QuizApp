Clementine Calm&tone Oat Face Cream

1200 DA

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

This cream is designed to calm, soothe and hydrate irritated or sensitive skin.

Clementine Calm & tone Face Moisturizer is a meticulously crafted and thoroughly researched skincare solution designed specifically for sensitive skin. We developed this original formula in-house, ensuring that every aspect is tailored to meet the unique needs of vulnerable skin.

Carefully selected ingredients, including oat, chamomile extract, panthenol, silk, and allantoin, are combined in precise proportions to create a truly customized moisturizer. Each ingredient has been meticulously studied for its soothing and nourishing properties, guaranteeing optimal performance and results.

This moisturizer embraces your skin in a veil of tranquility, providing the essential hydration your sensitive skin craves. With its lightweight and non-greasy formula, it effortlessly absorbs, leaving behind a fresh and rejuvenated feeling.

Clementine Calm & Tone Face Moisturizer:

  • Oat has soothing and anti-inflammatory properties, reducing redness and irritation. It also acts as a gentle exfoliator, promoting a smoother complexion by removing dead skin cells and impurities.
  • Vitamin B5 (panthenol) repairs the skin's barrier function, improving resilience and protecting against environmental stressors. It also aids in wound healing and helps to smooth the skin's texture.
  • Allantoin has soothing properties and helps to calm irritation and reduce redness and inflammation. It promotes cell regeneration, improving the overall appearance and texture of the skin. It also acts as a gentle exfoliator, unclogging pores and preventing breakouts.
  • Chamomile extract provides soothing and calming effects for sensitive skin. It offers antioxidant protection, reduces signs of aging, and moisturizes the skin. Chamomile acts as a gentle cleanser, promotes healing, and rejuvenates the skin.
  • Hyaluronic acid provides intense hydration by attracting and retaining moisture in the skin. It soothes sensitive skin, reducing redness and irritation. It also improves skin elasticity, minimizing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Its gentle nature makes it suitable for sensitive skin.

The moisturizer is fragrance-free and free from potential allergens, making it suitable for sensitive skin types.

Full ingredients list

Aqua, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Sodium hyaluronate, Prunus Armeniaca (Apricot) Kernel, Oil, Glycerin, Dicaprylyl Carbonate, Stearic acid, Cetearyl alcohol, Cetearyl Olivate (and) Sorbitan Olivate, Butyrospermum Parkii, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel flour, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel Oil, panthenol, carbomer, hydrolized silk, sodium chloride , sodium benzoate, potassium sorbate, tocopherol acetate, allantoin, citric acid, xanthan gum.

How to use

Start with a clean dry face, apply desired amount and massage gently using light motions until fully absorbed. For best results, use the Clementine Calm & Soothe Face Moisturizer twice daily as part of your skincare routine. And don’t forget to follow up with SPF during the day to provide protection. Enjoy the soothing and calming effects of the moisturizer as it works its magic, leaving your skin nourished, hydrated, and rejuvenated. Remember, consistency is key when it comes to skincare. Regular use of the Clementine Calm & Soothe Face Moisturizer will help maintain a balanced complexion and promote a serene, toned appearance.
