PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

This face wash is designed to deeply cleanse the sensitive skin and leave it soothed and hydrated.

Clementine Soothing Facial Cleanser is a gentle yet effective solution to remove grime without stripping the skin of its natural components. Carefully formulated with a blend of natural ingredients, including oat, chamomile extract, and aloe vera extract, this cleanser is specifically designed to provide soothing and nourishing care for vulnerable skin.

At Clementine, we prioritize extensive research and development to create skincare products that deliver exceptional results. We created this original formula in-house formula through obsessive research and dedication to provide the best solution for your sensitive skin needs.

The star ingredient, oat, possesses calming properties that help alleviate skin sensitivity, while chamomile extract works harmoniously to soothe and reduce redness, leaving your skin feeling balanced and comforted. The inclusion of aloe vera extract adds an extra layer of soothing hydration, promoting a refreshed and revitalized complexion.

Experience the perfect synergy of nature's gentle touch, cutting-edge research, and our in-house customization. The Clementine Soothing Facial Cleanser embodies the harmony of gentle care and exceptional results.

Clementine Calm & Tone Face Moisturizer:

This product is forumulated with very gentle surfactants to ensure utmost barrier protection, it contains fatty acid, oat and aloe vera extract to provide soothing and hydrating benefit on top.

  • Oats: Ideal for sensitive skin, oats offer soothing and anti-inflammatory properties that reduce redness and irritation. They moisturize the skin, maintaining hydration and balance. Oats gently exfoliate, revealing a smoother complexion by removing dead skin cells and impurities.
  • Chamomile: Chamomile extract provides soothing and calming effects for sensitive skin. It offers antioxidant protection and moisturizes the skin. Chamomile acts as a gentle cleanser, promotes healing, and rejuvenates the skin.
  • Aloe vera: Aloe vera extract offers soothing, hydrating, and healing benefits for sensitive skin. It calms inflammation, moisturizes the skin, and promotes the healing of damaged skin.

This product is free from all potential allergens such as fragrance.

Full ingredients list

Aqua, Aloe Barbadensis (aloe vera) Extract, Sodium cocoyl isethionate, Cocoamidopropylbetaine, Glycerin, Chamomilla Recutita (Matricaria) Flower Extract, Cetearyl alcohol, Stearic acid, Glyceryl Stearate SE, Avena Sativa (Oat) Kernel flour, Tocopherol acetate, sodium chloride , sodium benzoate, potassium sorbate, citric acid, xanthan gulm.

How to use

Take a small amount of the Clementine Soothing Facial Cleanser on a wet face and massage gently for about 1 to 2 minutes allowing it to effectively dissolve impurities and cleanse the pores. Rinse your face thoroughly with lukewarm water, ensuring that all traces of the cleanser are removed. Pat your face dry with a soft towel and avoid rubbing your skin harshly to prevent unnecessary irritation. For best results, follow up with the Clementine Calm & Tone Face Moisturizer to provide your skin with a nourishing and hydrating boost. Experience the gentle and soothing effects of the Clementine Soothing Facial Cleanser as it cleanses and cares for your sensitive skin. Consistent use will help maintain a refreshed and balanced complexion, revealing skin that feels clean, calm, and revitalized.
